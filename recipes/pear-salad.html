Pear Salad

Description

Estimated time: 5 minutes

Servings: 1-2 servings

Ingredients

  • 5oz bag of spring mix
  • 1 sliced pear
  • 1/2 cup of walnut pieces
  • 1/2 cup of goat cheese
  • 1 bottle of raspberry vinaigrette dressing

Steps

  1. Add the spring mix, pear, walnut pieces, and goat cheese together and mix them well
  2. Season the salad with the raspberry vinaigrette dressing to taste
